What This Assessment Covers

What This Automation Readiness Assessment Measures

This assessment looks at the signals that actually matter for automation readiness: repeatable delivery, recurring workload, tool sprawl, founder dependency, and operational waste. It is built for businesses that already have customers and want to know whether automation is the right next move.

Who This Is For

This assessment is best for agency owners, consultants, solo operators, and small service businesses with recurring work. If you are already making money but operations feel messy, this will give you a cleaner read on whether AI automation can help now.

What Happens After You Get Your Score

You will see whether your business is early, emerging, ready, or primed for automation. From there, the next move is simple: fix the fundamentals, run a self-guided audit, or apply for a paid automation audit to prioritize the highest-leverage systems.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an automation readiness assessment?
An automation readiness assessment is a quick evaluation of whether your business has the structure, repeatability, and operational pressure needed for automation to create real leverage.

How long does the assessment take?
About 3 minutes. It is designed to be fast enough to finish and useful enough to point you toward the right next step.

Is this assessment free?
Yes. The assessment is free. It gives you an instant score and recommended next step based on your answers.

Who should take this assessment?
Agency owners, consultants, solo operators, and service businesses with recurring work should take it if they suspect founder dependency or manual workflows are slowing growth.

What if my score is low?
A low score does not mean failure. It means your business probably needs more process clarity or offer repeatability before automation becomes high leverage. Start with the guide on signs your business is ready for AI automation if you need the context.
